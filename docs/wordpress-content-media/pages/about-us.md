## Valuatum

Valuatum is a software company that delivers credit risk and equity research solutions and systems to credit institutions, stockbrokers, investment banks, private equity companies and asset managers worldwide. We have provided solutions and systems for both buy-side and sell-side, as well as credit risk customers all across the world.

Founded by a team of former equity and credit risk analysts, we have a deep knowledge and understanding of financial markets. Our areas of expertise are credit risk analysis solutions, equity valuation models, reporting tools, interactive research libraries, and extranet solutions for portfolio managers and private equity investors.

We wanted to put our expertise to good use and provide credit risk assessments with our reports. The Valuatum Credit Risk Report gives a view about the financial state of a business and probability of credit default and bankruptcy. The report also offers an abundance of other information, such as a credit limit recommendation, key ratios and industry comparisons. The models we use are constantly trained with new data and relevant information in order to obtain as accurate estimates as possible.

We also offer a tool for small business valuation. Valuatum Estimator is an easy-to-use tool and provides a comprehensive estimate for the value of your company. It allows you to automatically generate company valuation reports and modify your own future scenarios. You can find more information about the Valuatum Estimator from its own website: [https://company-valuation.com/](https://company-valuation.com/)
