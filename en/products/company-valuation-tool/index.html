Company Valuation Tool

Reliable company value estimates with automatically generated forecasts and editable scenarios — for ownership changes, partnerships, and funding decisions.

What it offers

Understand the value of any Danish company

The Company Valuation Tool provides a reliable estimate for the value of a company, with reports generated through automatic estimates or user-modified scenarios. It draws on financial data from over 300,000 Danish companies via the Danish Central Business Register.

The tool is designed for use cases including ownership changes, partnerships, mergers and acquisitions, and funding evaluations — where a structured, data-driven valuation estimate provides a useful starting point for discussions.

Automated financial forecasts based on historical data
Editable forecast assumptions for scenario testing
Multiple valuation methods
