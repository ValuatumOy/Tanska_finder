Why CreditReports.dk

State-of-the-art machine learning for better credit decisions

Our XGBoost model is trained on extensive historical financial data and bankruptcy outcomes. Unlike traditional fixed-weight models, it dynamically adjusts variable importance based on each company's individual circumstances — consistently outperforming alternatives.

63%

Credit loss reduction

Our ML model reduced credit losses by up to 63% compared to traditional logistic regression in Finnish market testing on 200,000 companies.

94%

Bankruptcy prediction accuracy

Over 94% of bankruptcies occurred in the bottom 50% of companies by our credit score — 280× higher default rate in the lowest decile vs. highest.

30+

Dynamic financial variables

The model evaluates over 30 financial indicators — solvency, profitability, liquidity, industry risk, size — adjusting weights for each individual company.
