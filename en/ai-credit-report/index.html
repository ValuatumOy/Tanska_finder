What it is

Same credit risk data.
Better AI-assisted analysis.

The AI Credit Report builds on the same financial and credit risk data as the standard Credit Risk Report. The difference is the AI-assisted analysis layer: clearer written interpretation, qualitative observations, and a more decision-ready summary.

The report is designed for users who want more than raw figures — a clearer explanation of what the numbers mean, what risks matter, and what should be checked before making a credit decision.

It is delivered as a downloadable PDF and purchased on a per-report basis. No subscription required.

AI Credit Report includes

  • Financial statements and key ratios
  • Credit score and credit rating
  • Bankruptcy risk assessment
  • Credit limit recommendation
  • AI-assisted written analysis
  • Web-search-supported qualitative observations
  • Decision-ready narrative summary

Report structure

What the AI Credit Report contains

01

Company overview

Legal name, CVR number, industry, founding year, location, and key company facts.

02

Financial statement data

Income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow summary based on official Danish filings.

03

Key financial ratios

Profitability, solvency, liquidity, and efficiency ratios with trend comparison.

04

Credit score and credit rating

Quantitative credit score (0–100) and credit rating based on Valuatum's risk models.

05

Bankruptcy risk

Probability of default estimate derived from financial data and machine-learning models.

06

AI-assisted qualitative observations

Contextual observations on the company's business environment, based on web-search-supported AI analysis.

07

Written credit risk analysis

A clear, AI-written interpretation of the financial and credit risk data — explaining what the numbers mean.

08

Conclusion and decision-support summary

A concise summary of key risk factors and credit considerations to support the user's credit decision.
