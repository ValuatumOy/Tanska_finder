Credit Risk Platform Manual

Detailed documentation on how to use the platform features, generate estimates, and create credit risk reports.

The CreditReports.dk platform provides credit risk assessment tools for over 300,000 Danish companies. This manual covers the core features and workflows. For video walkthroughs, see the Platform Tutorials page.

General

Account setup and system orientation

Register at the Create Account page, confirm your email, and log in at platform.creditreports.dk. The platform is available free of charge during beta — no payment information required.
